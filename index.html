1. Cherries

Cherries are vibrant, juicy fruits renowned for their sweet-tart flavor and glossy, deep red hue. Belonging to the genus Prunus, they are members of the rose family and are closely related to plums, peaches, and almonds. Cherries come in two main varieties: sweet cherries, which are typically enjoyed fresh, and sour cherries, often used in cooking and baking.
